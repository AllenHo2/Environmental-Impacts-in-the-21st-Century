Methodology

Backgrounds and Motivations

Growing up I’ve always like studying about animals and environmental impacts, so I was very inspired to base my project on something that related to animals or environmental impacts. I was also very inspired by this dataset because there seems to be a lot of correlation between the different data that are being presented so I think I can make some pretty interesting data visualizations to connect all these datas.

Project Objectives:

  • Allow the audience to be able to see how environmental pollution affects humans
  • To see how these environmental impacts affect factors outside of our health such as our economy
  • See improvements that are being made to address these environmental impacts

    • Data Processing:

    After carefully reviewing a lot of the csv’s within this dataset, I think that a lot of the data is already sorted for me and that beside’s taking into consideration that there are sources inside the csv itself. I also used google sheets to help me transpose the data into my desired csv format

    Design:

    Bar Charts

  • Show the correlation between different hunting licence prices and Atmospheric Carbon Dioxide levels to show how environmental impacts affect the economy (Objective 2)

    • Line Charts

  • Shows the Average Air Quality experienced by a human to show how environmental pollution affects humans (Objective 1)

    • Parallel Line Chart

  • Show the positive correlation between health checked facilities in recent years to show improvements that are made in correlation with the ongoing pollutions (Objective 3)

    • Scatter Plot

  • Compare the new endangered species per year to greenhouse gas pollution in tons To show how environmental impacts endangers other animals/nature (Objective 2)
