Environmental Impacts in the 21st Century

Allen Ho

Purpose

This project aims to evaluate and compare trends of environmental impacts that had been made in the 21st century. The dataset used in this project comes from https://usafacts.org/data/topics/security-safety/energy-and-environment/ which provides data on the environmental impacts of the United States. The dataset contains various aspects of environmental impacts that we will explore and analyze through upcoming data visualizations.
