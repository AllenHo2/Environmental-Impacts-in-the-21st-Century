Environmental Impacts in the 21st Century

Purpose

Evaluate and compare trends of environmental impacts that had been made in the 21st century.

Bar Charts comparing Fossil Fuels to Carbon Levels

The continuing trend of increasing carbon levels is almost a direct result of the increasing use of fossil fuels.
